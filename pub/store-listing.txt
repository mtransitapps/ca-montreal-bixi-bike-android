Full description:
This application adds Montreal BIXI bike stations information to MonTransit.

This app contains the bike stations position & availability as well as the latest news from montreal.bixi.com and @BIXImontreal on Twitter.

BIXI Montreal is available from April to November in Montreal, Westmount and Longueuil.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bike stations information (number of available bikes & docks).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from a web-service provided by BIXI Montreal: 
https://montreal.bixi.com/data/bikeStations.xml

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-montreal-bixi-bike-android/

This application is not related with the BIXI Montreal.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load bike stations information and to read news on montreal.bixi.com and Twitter.
