This application adds Montreal BIXI bike information in MT.

Once this application is installed, the MT app will display bike stations information (number of available bikes & docks).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MT app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5m

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from a web-service provided by BIXI Montreal: 
https://montreal.bixi.com/data/bikeStations.xml

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-montreal-bixi-bike-android/
